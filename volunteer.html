Volunteer Crew

Every crew is led by volunteers elected in their crews to lead diverse persons from all works of life- citizens of different countries, professionals, academicians, captains of industries, the young and the old. Crew leaders are called CREW CAPTAINS.

How cool is that? Except that they combine fun and earnest as they lead their teams to navigate difficult circumstances.

Make a difference as you join us to:

Take action

Create a community of people who believe in social justice and human rights

Make an impact

Help empower others

Make real connections through networking.

Human Rights Crew

This Crew speaks to the core of what House of Justice™ is about (promotion and protection of human rights). Are you interested in taking up human and civic rights causes? Do you want to speak for the oppressed? Look no further than this group.

Molluma Medico - Legal Crew

Volunteers of these Crew report incidents of medical negligence. They also create awareness and events on medical negligence and work to catalogue victims of mass atrocities for the ends of justice. Health personnel, victims of medical negligence, forensic experts and enthusiasts and those who are interested in accountability for victims of mass atrocities will find this crew a place to be.

Crime prevention and Counselling Crew

The world can be such a cold place when challenges are braced all alone. This crew comprises of volunteer counsellors, psychologists, psychiatrists and those with the milk of human kindness who want to reach out and reach back. You can join this crew of people who definitely show that the world can be warm and loving.

House of Justice Media & Publicity Crew

This Crew engages in public enlightenment programs for the good of society. If you like to educate the public on pertinent issues, we recommend this group to you.

Logistics & Planning Crew

With all the events that take place in House of Justice™, this crew is responsible for making everything come together. You are welcome to serve!

Street & School Crew

The street creates its own heroes. School dropouts, young people experimenting on drugs, lack of quality and relevant education…all that and more is what this crew seeks to address. The Street & School crew is one of the machineries the House of Justice is exploring to bridge the dream gaps between rural and urban children. Interested? You are right there!

ICT & Sound Crew

Mind-blowing images, amazing jingles, picture quotes…what are we missing? They make pictures and sounds that are worth billions of words! Join this crew if you have the skills and flair!

Finance Crew

This Crew works to make money a tool for service for humanity. Interested in fundraising for human causes? You are right home with this Crew.
